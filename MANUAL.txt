CAM — USER MANUAL
=================


CAMERA MODE
-----------
The app opens in camera mode showing a live camera feed.

  Settings button (bottom left)   — opens settings
  Shutter button  (bottom centre) — takes a photo
  Switch camera   (bottom right)  — toggles front/back camera

After taking a photo, an overlay shows the captured image.
  Download button (top left of overlay)  — saves the photo
  Close button    (top right of overlay) — returns to camera


FIBONACCI SQUARES MODE
----------------------
Press the button in the top right corner to enter Fibonacci mode.
The bottom bar is hidden. The live camera feed stays visible.

-- Placing the first square --
A guide square appears in the centre of the screen.
Tap anywhere to place the centre of the first square.

-- Placing the second square --
Tap on any of the 4 sides of the first square to place the second
square. The direction you choose determines the orientation of the
spiral.

-- Adding more squares --
Each tap adds the next Fibonacci square. Squares grow following the
Fibonacci sequence (1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13 ...) and automatically
arrange into a spiral.

-- Controls (bottom centre) --
  −        decrease the size of the active spiral
  +        increase the size of the active spiral
  ↻        rotate the spiral direction 90° (cycles through 4 directions)
  new      freeze the current spiral and start a new one
  del      delete the active spiral

-- Spiral list (top left, appears when 2+ spirals exist) --
Spirals are labelled A, B, C ...
Tap a label to select and edit that spiral.
The active spiral is shown at full brightness; others are dimmed.
Deleting a spiral keeps the remaining labels unchanged
(e.g. deleting A from [A, B, C] leaves [B, C]).

-- Moving a spiral --
Press and hold within the area of the first two squares (the centre
of the spiral) and drag to reposition it.

-- Taking a photo --
Press the shutter button (top centre) to capture the scene.
The spiral overlays are NOT included in the photo — only the camera
image is captured.
